How to Add Your Photos

1. Replace the image URLs in the HTML code with your own image URLs

2. Update the alt text and captions for accessibility

3. For best results, use images with a 3:4 aspect ratio (like 300x400px)

4. You can add more photo items by copying the structure of existing ones
